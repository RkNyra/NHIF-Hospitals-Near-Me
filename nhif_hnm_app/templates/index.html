About/How to use this app

This app was made with you in mind...to make it easier for you to find the NHIF-registered hospitals near you/in your location. To make the most of this app, just follow the steps below:

  1. Click the find a hospital link on the navbar to be navigated to the search page,
  2. You'll then select a location so as to retrieve a list of hospitals in that location,
  3. You can then select a hospital from your search results so as to view more information about it.
  4. You can also register or login so that you can see hospital reviews or share your own experience/review of a hospital you visited.

Brief NHIF Overview

National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) is a State Parastatal whose core mandate is to provide medical insurance cover to all its members and their declared dependants (spouse and children). The NHIF membership is open to all Kenyans who have attained the age of 18 years and years and have a monthly income of more than Ksh 1000. If you'd like to know more about NHIF, click here.
